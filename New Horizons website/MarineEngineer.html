Want to be a Marine Engineer?

Who are they?
Marine Engineers are the ones that:

  • ➼ Design
  • ➼ Build
  • ➼ And maintain the machinery used on offshore vessels and rigs.

What do they do?
Marine Engineers are in charge of ensuring the:

  • ➼ mechanical
  • ➼ electrical
  • ➼ and hydraulic systems on offshore platforms operate efficiently.

These are the CSEC and CAPE subjects needed:
CSEC

  • ➼ Physics
  • ➼ Mathematics
  • ➼ Technical Drawing
  • ➼ Information Technology
CAPE
  • ➼ Pure Mathematics
  • ➼ Physics
  • ➼ Mechanical Engineering
  • ➼ Electrical Technology

Here are the other skills you would need:

  • ➼ Mechanical Engineering
  • ➼ Hydraulics
  • ➼ Troubleshooting
  • ➼ Computer-aided design (CAD)

This career is compatible with you if you have the following personality traits:

  • ➼ Practical
  • ➼ Problem-solving
  • ➼ Adaptable
  • ➼ Disciplined

You would be required to be familiarized with the following tech

  • ➼ CAD software: For designing marine machinery
  • ➼ Engine monitoring systems: Used to track the performance of engines and mechanical systems.
  • ➼ Hydraulic systems: Knowledge of hydraulic equipment maintenance and operation.
