Want to be a Health, Safety and Equipment (HSE) Supervisior?

Who are they?
Health, Safety and Equipment (HSE) Supervisiors are the ones that:

  • Ensure that oil and gas operations comply with health, safety, and environmental regulations.

What do they do?
HSE Supervisors are in charge of:

  • ➼ Conducting risk assessments
  • ➼ Enforcing safety protocols
  • ➼ Ensuring hydraulic systems on offshore platforms operate efficiently.

These are the CSEC and CAPE subjects needed:
CSEC

  • ➼ Chemistry
  • ➼ Mathematics
  • ➼ Biology
  • ➼ Information Technology
CAPE
  • ➼ Environmental Science
  • ➼ Chemistry
  • ➼ Pure Mathematics
  • ➼ Management of Business

Here are the other skills you would need:

  • ➼ Strong observation
  • ➼ Data analysis
  • ➼ Knowledge of regulations
  • ➼ Communication

This career is compatible with you if you have the following personality traits:

  • ➼ Detail-oriented
  • ➼ Responsible
  • ➼ Proactive
  • ➼ Ethical

You would be required to be familiarized with the following tech

  • ➼ Incident management software: Used to log and manage safety incidents
  • ➼ Environmental monitoring tools: Technology for tracking emissions, pollutants
  • ➼ Safety audit software: For tracking compliance with safety regulations
