Want to be a Petrol Engineer?

Who are they?
Petrol Engineers are speacialists that:

  • Design and develop methods for extracting oil and gas from underground reservoirs.

What do they do?
Petrol Engineers are in charge of:

  • ➼ Planning and supervising drilling operations
  • ➼ Ensuring efficiency and safety in extracting resources

These are the CSEC and CAPE subjects needed:
CSEC

  • ➼ Physics
  • ➼ Mathematics
  • ➼ Chemistry
  • ➼ Information Technology
CAPE
  • ➼ Pure Mathematics
  • ➼ Physics
  • ➼ Chemistry
  • ➼ Information Technology

Here are the other skills you would need:

  • ➼ Strong analytical skills
  • ➼ Problem-solving
  • ➼ Numerical skills
  • ➼ Programming
  • ➼ Data analysis

This career is compatible with you if you have the following personality traits:

  • ➼ Detail-oriented
  • ➼ Logical thinker
  • ➼ Collaborative
  • ➼ Innovative

You would be required to be familiarized with the following tech

  • ➼ Reservoir simulation software: Models how oil flows through reservoirs.
  • ➼ Drilling equipment: Understanding of modern drilling technologies.
  • ➼ Data analysis tools: Use software like MATLAB or Python for data interpretation.
