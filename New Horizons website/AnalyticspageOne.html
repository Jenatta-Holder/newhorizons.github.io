Salary and Working Hours

An HSE Supervisor working in Guyana will typically earn around 1,333,900 GYD per year, and this can range from the lowest average salary of about 692,500 GYD to the highest average salary of 2,038,500 GYD.

Working hours are typically flexible to ensure duties are carried out. A typical working week would contain 40 hours

Salary for Experience

  • 0 - 2 Years Experience. An HSE Supervisor in Guyana that has less than two years of experience can expect to earn somewhere in the region of 785,400 GYD.
  • 2 - 5 Years Experience. With two to five years of experience the average HSE Supervisor salary would increase to 1,053,900 GYD.
  • 5 - 10 Years Experience. From five to ten years of experience as an HSE Supervisor, the average salary would be 1,369,700 GYD.
  • 10 - 15 Years Experience. Once you have more than ten years of experience the average salary reaches around 1,655,500 GYD.
  • 15 - 20 Years Experience. An HSE Supervisor with 15 to 20 years of experience can earn an average of 1,811,000 GYD.
  • 20+ Years Experience. For an HSE Supervisor with more than 20 years, the expected average salary increases to 1,908,800 GYD.

Salary for Education Levels

  • Bachelor's Degree. An HSE Supervisor in Guyana with a bachelor's degree education can earn an average salary of 1,108,500 GYD.
  • Master's Degree. An HSE Supervisor in Guyana with a master's degree education can earn an average salary of 1,537,500 GYD.

Salary for Gender

In Guyana, a male hse supervisor will earn an average of 1,391,600 GYD, while a female hse supervisor will earn around 1,283,600 GYD.
This means that a male hse supervisor earns approximately 8% more than a female hse supervisor for performing the same job.
