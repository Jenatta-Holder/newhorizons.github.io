Salary and Working Hours

A Drilling Engineer working in Guyana will typically earn around 1,800,200 GYD per year, and this can range from the lowest average salary of about 828,400 GYD to the highest average salary of 2,868,600 GYD.

Most work more than 40 hours a week. May work seven days a week, if working at a drill site as a drilling engineer. May be on-call in case of emergencies or changes in drilling conditions. May travel to remote areas and stay there for extended periods.

Salary for Experience

  • 0 - 2 Years Experience - A Drilling Engineer in Guyana that has less than two years of experience can expect to earn somewhere in the region of 939,600 GYD.
  • 2 - 5 Years Experience - With two to five years of experience the average Drilling Engineer salary would increase to 1,259,300 GYD.
  • 5 - 10 Years Experience. From five to ten years of experience as a Drilling Engineer, the average salary would be 1,858,200 GYD.
  • 10 - 15 Years Experience. Once you have more than ten years of experience the average salary reaches around 2,266,400 GYD.
  • 15 - 20 Years Experience. A Drilling Engineer with 15 to 20 years of experience can earn an average of 2,471,700 GYD.
  • 20+ Years Experience. For a Drilling Engineer with more than 20 years, the expected average salary increases to 2,676,200 GYD.

Salary for Education Levels

  • Certificate or Diploma. A Drilling Engineer in Guyana with a certificate or diploma education can earn an average salary of 1,074,600 GYD.
  • Bachelor's Degree. A Drilling Engineer in Guyana with a bachelor's degree education can earn an average salary of 1,678,300 GYD.
  • Master's Degree. A Drilling Engineer in Guyana with a master's degree education can earn an average salary of 2,819,600 GYD.

Salary for Gender

In Guyana, a male drilling engineer will earn an average of 1,896,700 GYD, while a female drilling engineer will earn around 1,703,200 GYD.
This means that a male drilling engineer earns approximately 11% more than a female drilling engineer for performing the same job.
