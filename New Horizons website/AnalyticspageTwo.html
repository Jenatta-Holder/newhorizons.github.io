Salary and Working Hours

A Marine Engineer working in Guyana will typically earn around 1,846,200 GYD per year, and this can range from the lowest average salary of about 955,800 GYD to the highest average salary of 2,819,600 GYD.

In modern vessels, with an unmanned engineroom, the engineers work day work 12 hours, on and 12 hours off. Typically 06:00 to 18:00hrs. During the night a 'duty' engineer is designated to answer engine alarms.

Salary for Experience

  • 0 - 2 Years Experience. A Marine Engineer in Guyana that has less than two years of experience can expect to earn somewhere in the region of 1,088,800 GYD.
  • 2 - 5 Years Experience. With two to five years of experience the average Marine Engineer salary would increase to 1,464,200 GYD.
  • 5 - 10 Years Experience. From five to ten years of experience as a Marine Engineer, the average salary would be 1,896,700 GYD.
  • 10 - 15 Years Experience. Once you have more than ten years of experience the average salary reaches around 2,290,300 GYD.
  • 15 - 20 Years Experience. A Marine Engineer with 15 to 20 years of experience can earn an average of 2,508,300 GYD.
  • 20+ Years Experience. For a Marine Engineer with more than 20 years, the expected average salary increases to 2,641,300 GYD.

Salary for Education Levels

  • Bachelor's Degree. A Marine Engineer in Guyana with a bachelor's degree education can earn an average salary of 1,537,500 GYD.
  • Master's Degree. A Marine Engineer in Guyana with a master's degree education can earn an average salary of 2,136,200 GYD.

Salary for Gender

In Guyana, a male marine engineer will earn an average of 1,921,500 GYD, while a female marine engineer will earn around 1,788,300 GYD.
This means that a male marine engineer earns approximately 7% more than a female marine engineer for performing the same job.
