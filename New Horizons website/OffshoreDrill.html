Want to be an Offshore Drilling Engineer?

Who are they?
Offshore Drilling Engineers are the ones that:

  • Manage the technical aspects of drilling in offshore oil rigs.

What do they do?
Offshore Drilling Engineers are the ones that:

  • Oversee operations to ensure safe and efficient extraction of oil and gas in deep-sea environments.

These are the CSEC and CAPE subjects needed:
CSEC

  • ➼ Physics
  • ➼ Mathematics
  • ➼ Technical Drawing
  • ➼ Chemistry
CAPE
  • ➼ Pure Mathematics
  • ➼ Physics
  • ➼ Chemistry
  • ➼ Technical Drawing

Here are the other skills you would need:

  • ➼ Strong mathematics
  • ➼ Computer-aided design (CAD)
  • ➼ Mechanical reasoning
  • ➼ Project management

This career is compatible with you if you have the following personality traits:

  • ➼ Resilient
  • ➼ Hands-on
  • ➼ Quick decision maker
  • ➼ Safety-conscious

You would be required to be familiarized with the following tech

  • ➼ CAD software: Used for designing drilling systems
  • ➼ Rigs and subsea equipment: Knowledge of offshore rig technologies
  • ➼ SCADA systems: Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition systems for monitoring operations
